Core Pillars

Deliver high-performing experiences

Lay a reliable technical base so every future campaign compounds.

Performance

Lightning-Fast Load Times

Slow websites frustrate users and rank lower. Google uses Core Web Vitals to assess page experience—optimize images, minify code, and rely on caching/CDNs to protect conversion rates and reduce bounce.

Experience

Mobile-First Design

The majority of sessions now happen on phones, and Google indexes mobile versions first. Design for smaller screens, streamline navigation, and ensure no critical content disappears when the viewport shrinks.

Crawlability

Indexing & Site Architecture

XML sitemaps, clean robots.txt rules, and logical navigation paths help bots discover pages quickly. Remove dead ends, audit coverage in Search Console, and fix broken links before publishing new sections.

Rich Results

Structured Data

Schema.org markup clarifies content types for search engines. Use JSON-LD, keep it consistent with visible content, and validate with Google’s Rich Results Test to unlock reviews, FAQs, and product highlights.

Strategy & Authority

Content that matches search intent

Blend research, experience, and trust signals to win the SERP.

Research

Keyword Research

Start with the phrases your customers use. Target long-tail opportunities that map to intent (informational, transactional, local) and turn them into topic clusters with supporting articles and internal links.

Content

Quality & Experience

Craft authoritative, in-depth pages that answer the task completely. Incorporate expertise, support with visuals, and format content for easy scanning. Remember Google’s emphasis on E-E-A-T signals.

Authority

Link Building

Earn links through partnerships, PR, and shareable assets. Focus on relevance and trust—quality links reinforce topical authority and help new pages index faster than any quick-win tactic ever will.

Tool Stack

Essential SEO Tools

Use lightweight diagnostics weekly and deeper research monthly.

  • Google Search Console: monitor indexing status, coverage, and Core Web Vitals alerts.
  • Google Analytics: track traffic sources, engagement, and conversion lift from organic search.
  • PageSpeed Insights: lab + field data for performance fixes without guessing.
  • Ahrefs / SEMrush: conduct keyword research, backlink analysis, and competitor benchmarking.
  • Surfer, Clearscope, or similar: enrich content outlines with semantic terms readers expect.
  • Looker Studio dashboards: visualize how SEO contributes to revenue and pipeline.

Key KPIs to Watch

  • Page Speed & Core Web Vitals

    Frequent testing keeps LCP, FID, and CLS within target budgets.

  • Organic Traffic Growth

    Measure net new sessions and the mix of branded vs. non-branded terms.

  • Keyword Rankings

    Track primary and supporting keywords to spot volatility early.

  • Conversion & Revenue Impact

    Report on leads, purchases, or bookings attributed to search.

  • Engagement Signals

    Monitor bounce rate, time-on-page, and scroll depth for UX issues.

  • Backlink Quality

    Audit referring domains and disavow spam to keep authority clean.

  • Technical Health

    Use regular crawls to find broken links, duplicate content, or thin pages.
